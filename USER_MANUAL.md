# User Manual: Pag-Ambit Interactive Web Comic

## Welcome to Pag-Ambit!

**Pag-Ambit** is an immersive digital comic book experience that brings stories to life with realistic page-turning effects, interactive multimedia, and a responsive design. Whether you are reading on a desktop or a mobile device, this manual will guide you through all the features available to enhance your reading experience.

## üöÄ Getting Started

1.  **Open the Application**: Navigate to the web address provided for the comic book.
2.  **Loading**: Wait a brief moment for the book and its resources (images, sounds) to load.
3.  **Start Reading**: You will see the cover page. You can start reading immediately!

## üìñ Navigation Controls

There are multiple ways to turn pages and navigate through the story:

### 1. Mouse & Touch Interactions
*   **Click/Tap**: Click on the **right edge** of a page to turn forward, or the **left edge** to turn backward.
*   **Swipe (Mobile/Touch)**: Swipe **left** to go to the next page, or **right** to go to the previous page.

### 2. On-Screen Buttons
At the bottom of the screen, you will find the control bar:
*   **`< Prev`**: Go to the previous page.
*   **`Next >`**: Go to the next page.
*   **`Cover`**: Jump instantly to the front cover.
*   **`End`**: Jump instantly to the back cover.

### 3. Keyboard Shortcuts
For a seamless reading experience on desktop:
*   **Right Arrow (‚Üí)**: Next page.
*   **Left Arrow (‚Üê)**: Previous page.

### 4. Table of Contents
*   Click the **List Icon** (three lines) in the control bar to open the **Table of Contents**.
*   Select a chapter or scene to jump directly to that page.

Enhance your reading experience with these tools found in the control bar:

### üîç Zoom & Magnification

### üåô View Modes

*   **Fullscreen**: Click the **Expand Icon** to enter fullscreen mode for a distraction-free experience.

### üîä Audio Control
*   **Sound Effects**: The book features realistic page-turn sounds.

## ‚ú® Interactive Features

**Pag-Ambit** is more than just images!

*   **Speech Bubbles**: Hover your mouse over characters or scenes to reveal hidden dialogue and thoughts.
*   **Video Panels**: Some panels are animated video loops (cinemagraphs) that bring scenes to life.
*   **Notes**: Click the **Note Icon** to open a quick menu for your favorite note-taking apps (Google Keep, Notion, etc.) to jot down theories or thoughts.
*   **Share**: Click the **Share Icon** to copy the link or share your current read on social media.
*   **Save/Download**:
    *   **Save Page**: Download the current page as an image.
    *   **Download PDF**: (If available) Download the entire comic for offline reading.

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

*   **Pages not loading?**: Check your internet connection and refresh the page.
*   **Sound not playing?**: Ensure your device volume is up and the Mute button in the app is not active. Note that some browsers require a user interaction (click/tap) before allowing audio to play.
*   **Layout looks wrong?**: Try resizing your browser window or switching to/from Fullscreen mode.
